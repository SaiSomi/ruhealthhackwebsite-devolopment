Join the Rutgers Health Hackathon and be a part of revolutionizing healthcare. Collaborate, innovate, and create solutions that will shape the future of healthcare.

Discover Our 2024 Hackathon Themes!

Explore the themes that drive our hackathon, designed to address critical challenges in healthcare.

Global Health

This theme focuses on developing innovative solutions to eliminate disparities in healthcare access and outcomes, ensuring high-quality care is accessible to all, regardless of geography, socioeconomic status, or ethnicity.

Innovation in Medical Education

This theme focuses on revolutionizing medical education through innovative tools and methods that enhance learning, making it more interactive, comprehensive, and accessible for future healthcare professionals.

Patient Outcomes & Safety

This theme centers on developing innovative technologies and strategies to enhance patient care and safety, optimize health outcomes through personalized care, and prevent medical errors by leveraging data and predictive analytics.

Hospital Operational Excellence

This theme focuses on developing innovative solutions to streamline healthcare operations, reduce costs, and improve service delivery by optimizing workflows and resource management.
